About Verifreight

The trucking industry runs on paperwork — and that paperwork slows everything down. Brokers spend hours chasing carriers for signatures, following up on missing documents, and manually tracking compliance status across dozens of companies.

We give freight brokers a single platform to send carrier packets, collect signatures, and approve or reject documents — all without a single phone call or email chain. Carriers receive a simple signing link, sign in minutes, and brokers get notified instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Verifreight

How does Verifreight work?

+

Verifreight streamlines the carrier onboarding process by providing a digital platform for sending, signing, and approving documents. Brokers send packet links to carriers, who sign digitally, and brokers receive instant notifications for approval.

Is it legally binding?

+

Yes, all electronic signatures collected through Verifreightr are legally binding and compliant with the E-Sign Act and UETA regulations.

How secure is my data?

+

We use bank-level encryption to protect all documents and data. Everything is stored securely in the cloud with automatic backups and full audit trails.

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

+

Yes, our Professional and Enterprise plans include API access for seamless integration with your existing TMS and other business systems.

What kind of support do you offer?

+

All plans include email support. Professional plans get priority support, and Enterprise customers receive dedicated account management and 24/7 phone support.
